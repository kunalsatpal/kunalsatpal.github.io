Overview

To ensure Cure.fit platform has enough good quality supply of partners there was a need to follow stringent partner identification and verification process before they are onboarded onto our platform.

Currently the entire process is managed via Zoho CRM. While this streamlines the backend process, partner facing experience is still broken as they get multiple communication through various touch points without giving a coherent onboarding flow.

Problem Statement

Improving the overall onboarding process and make it much more faster and self reliant with minimal need of manual processes.

Goals/Key success metrics:
  1. Reduce Turn around time(TAT) from 15-18 to 7-9 days for Fitness partners and from 7-9 to 2~3 days for Health Partners.
  2. Reduce lead management team size from 21 to 3~5 members.

About the user

Unlike our cure.fit's customer base, partners have a very different persona and very different needs from the customers. Hence it is essential to have a proper understanding of them before moving on.


Deep analysis of the current process

Landing page, multiple big forms, a lot of manual interventions and emails mainly framed the overall flow. Here is a detailed customer jouney map with all the major user touch points.


Major challenges to tackle

From the detailed analysis of the flow we scoped what are the problems to be solved.

  1. Help users know more about what they are getting into, more about cure.fit and partners at cure.fit before onboarding
  2. Help users understand the overall onboarding process
  3. Help users complete the process without any need to contact support
  4. Speed up the process by reducing manual interventions
  5. Reduce anxiety amoung users during waiting periods
  6. Make sure everything is mobile compatible
Setting principles to follow
Before starting it was essential to set some basic principles to look back at when things get complicated.
Working on challenges

All flows not included in the case study and focussed on major goals. You can check the complete platform at Partner.fit and flow chart at Flow chart link

1. Help users know more and make a decision

Its essential to answer all the questions and user queries to make usure they are confideent about the marketplace. Where Fitness marketplace relies lot on users inner desire to help the society and create an impact.

2. Help users understand the overall onboarding process

A lack of understanding of the process created multiple other problems and anxiety among users. Thus became one of the most crucial problems to solve for. It is important to make sure user is aware of the overall process and doen't feel cheated or played with later in the process.

3. Help users complete the overall process

A long and tiring process with multiple sub-process leads to multiple questions among users, thus it is essential that all the questions are answered right away and raise minimum queries.

4. Removing all manual steps and speeding up the process

A lot or time and manual effort goes into finding a right interview slot. Multiple back and forth's between the panel and the candidate delays the overall process.

5. Helping users understand there candidature state and reduce axiety amoung them

A lot of tickets were fom confused users, who had no idea about there status if they needs to do something, if there is a waiting period, how long might be the waiting period, if their candidature is closed or something else?


6. Make sure everything is Mobile compatible

50% percent of users were using mobile devices earlier, thus it was essential that the whole portal is mobile friendly.

The Launch

After a few months of development, Parnter.fit finally launched in December 2020 and currently live at https://partner.cure.fit/

What went well

Most of the key results were met in a month of launch. More data is being collected to make incremental improvements and make the experience even better.
